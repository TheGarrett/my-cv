About Me

Ryan Garrett

Frontend Developer

Background:

Growing up I always had strong interests in technology which made an impact on the subjects I picked at school and college as I wanted to explore how they could help me be creative, this led me to studying IT & Art. IT taught me the core skills of website building and the fundamentals of website design where as Art allowed me to show my creative side. My art projects mainly focused on photo editing and digital art.

My hobby outside of school was video editing for my YouTube channel. I’d tend to do music videos by mixing Movies/TV shows with what was known then as “Dubstep”. I would also edit the videos for my other YouTuber friends when I had the time.

More recently I have spent my last 5 years of working for Accenture as a frontend developer. I joined as an apprentice which meant I was required to complete my NVQ, college and university studies while working full time at Accenture. During this 5 years I’ve picked up a vast amount of skills such as problem solving, which includes analysing and deconstructing complex requirements to create and deliver solutions. Utilising relevant techniques and methodologies to support and execute the design, build, and maintenance of applications and assets.

Outside of work I try to keep myself active by going to the gym or playing pool with my friends. If I’m not being active then I try to continue my growth by upskilling myself in UX/UI by doing online courses. With my past skills from Accenture, University and college, I feel this would be a great career path to go down.
